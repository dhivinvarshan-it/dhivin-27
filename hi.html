Name of The Bikes

YAMAHA MT15

The Yamaha MT-15 is a naked street motorcycle manufactured by Yamaha Motor Company.
It was first introduced in India in March 2019.
The bike is powered by a 155cc liquid-cooled, 4-stroke, single-cylinder engine.
The engine produces a maximum power of 18.5 bhp at 10,000 rpm and a maximum torque of 13.9 Nm at 8,500 rpm.
The bike has a 6-speed gearbox and a top speed of around 130 km/h.
It features a fully-digital LCD instrument cluster, LED headlamp and taillamp, and a muscular tank design.
The MT-15 has a lightweight and compact frame, which makes it easy to maneuver in city traffic and on twisty roads.

YAMAHA R15 V4

The Yamaha YZF-R15 V4 is a fully-faired sport bike that was launched in India in December 2021.
It is powered by a 155cc liquid-cooled, 4-stroke, single-cylinder engine that produces a maximum power of 18.6 bhp at 10,000 rpm and a maximum torque of 14.1 Nm at 8,500 rpm.
The bike has a 6-speed gearbox and a top speed of around 142 km/h.
It features a sleek and aerodynamic design, with a dual LED headlamp setup, LED taillights, and a digital instrument cluster.
The YZF-R15 V4 has a delta box frame, which provides excellent stability and handling, and a monoshock suspension at the rear for better comfort and performance.
It also comes with a slipper clutch, which reduces engine braking and provides smoother downshifts, as well as dual-channel ABS for better safety on the road.

YAMAHA FZ V4

The Yamaha FZ V4 is a popular naked street motorcycle manufactured by Yamaha Motor Company.
It was launched in India in October 2021.
The bike is powered by a 149cc air-cooled, 4-stroke, single-cylinder engine that produces a maximum power of 12.4 bhp at 7,250 rpm and a maximum torque of 13.3 Nm at 5,500 rpm.
The engine is mated to a 5-speed gearbox and has a top speed of around 112 km/h.
The FZ V4 features a new muscular design with a LED headlight, LED taillight, and a fully digital instrument cluster.
The bike has a diamond-type frame, which provides better rigidity and stability on the road, and a telescopic fork suspension at the front and a mono-shock suspension at the rear for better comfort and performance.
